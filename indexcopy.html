ABOUT SK BARANGAY MANKILAM

The Sangguniang Kabataan of Barangay Mankilam is dedicated to empowering the youth and fostering community development through leadership, service, and innovative programs.

Mission

To inspire and mobilize the youth of Barangay Mankilam towards active participation in community building, leadership, and social responsibility.

Vision

A progressive and united youth sector that leads positive change and contributes to the sustainable development of Barangay Mankilam.

Goal

To create opportunities and platforms for the holistic development of the youth, enabling them to become responsible, productive, and empowered members of the community.

Objectives
  • Promote youth participation in governance and community programs.
  • Develop leadership, social, and entrepreneurial skills among the youth.
  • Advocate for health, education, and environmental initiatives.
  • Foster unity, volunteerism, and civic responsibility.

NEWS & ANNOUNCEMENTS

September 13, 2024
Mankilam Youth Festival 2024
The Mankilam Youth Festival 2024 was a fun event filled with music, dance, games, and art. It was held at the Mankilam Covered Court and joined by many young people. The goal was to show the talents of the youth and bring everyone together.
April 28, 2025
YES 2025 Youth Environmental Summit – Davao Region
The SK Council joined the YES 2025 Summit in Davao Region. Youth leaders gathered to learn about protecting the environment, planting trees, and planning climate actions. It was a great day of learning and teamwork for a cleaner and greener future!
April 22- May 24, 2025
Barangay Youth Orientation – Earth Festival 10th Edition (2025)
The SK held a youth orientation for the 10th Earth Festival 2025 at the barangay hall. The event taught young people how to care for the environment and encouraged them to join activities like tree planting, clean-up drives, and recycling. Over 35 youth attended the orientation.
June 11, 2024
Binibining Mankilam 2024 Tree Planting Activity
The candidates of Binibining Mankilam 2024 joined a tree planting activity to help protect and care for the environment. This event was held to promote love for nature and to encourage the youth to take part in keeping our community green and clean.
March 8, 2024
SK Barangay Mankilam 6th Regular Meeting
The Sangguniang Kabataan of Barangay Mankilam held its 6th regular meeting to talk about important plans, upcoming youth events, and updates on ongoing projects.
March 8, 2024
Binibining Barangay Mankilam 2024
The Binibining Barangay Mankilam 2024 joined the parade with pride and beauty. She wore her sash and crown while waving to the crowd.
March 8, 2024
Basketball Players Under 39 of SK Barangay Mankilam
The SK Barangay Mankilam proudly supports 39 young basketball players. These players are part of the barangay’s basketball team. They practice hard and play with great teamwork.
