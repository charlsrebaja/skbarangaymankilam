SK BARANGAY MANKILAM PROJECTS & PROGRAM

Empowering the youth through meaningful programs and community engagement.

Donation Drive

As part of our ongoing donation drive, we aim to supply paint materials to public schools in need.

Youth Sports Festival

Annual inter-purok competitions that promote fitness, teamwork, and camaraderie among the youth.

Leadership Training

Empowering future youth leaders with essential skills and values. Join our workshops and seminars for personal and community growth.

Livelihood Program

Skills training and small business support for youth and families to promote sustainable income and entrepreneurship in the barangay.

Scholarship Program

Educational assistance for deserving youth. Apply at the barangay hall for requirements and screening.

Tree Planting

Barangay-wide tree planting activities to promote environmental awareness and sustainability.
